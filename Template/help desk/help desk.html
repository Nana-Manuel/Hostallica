STEPS TO BOOK ROOM

1. To book a room go to the book room page. This can be accessed by clicking on the book room link on the home page.

2. Select the hall of preference

3. Select floor and room preferred. Make sure to confirm the booking and proceed to the payment page.

4. Payments can be made based on the provided options.

WARNING

Payments are not refundable. Rooms would be lost after the 48hrs of grace period. Rooms canceled would attract a fee of Gh50 on the next booking.
